The Problem

Most training tells you what to do.
San4 makes you actually do it.

😰

You go blank

Five things to say. The meeting starts. You remember two.

▾
  • Adrenaline shuts down the sentence-forming part of your brain when stakes are high
  • You rehearsed answers, not real back-and-forth conversations under pressure
  • Without live pressure reps, confidence simply cannot be built before the day
🔇

No real feedback

"Be more confident" is not something you can act on.

▾
  • "Speak slower" gives you nothing concrete to actually practise
  • You cannot hear yourself the way a room hears you
  • One bad round quietly shakes confidence for the next three interactions
🌍

Wrong tools

Every coaching app was built for California, not Bengaluru.

▾
  • Calibrated for US English and American interview formats only
  • No scenarios for Indian MNCs, B-school GDs, or salary negotiations in ₹
  • Dollar pricing, Western examples, and zero local cultural context
